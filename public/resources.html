Where did your passion for coding start:

My passion for the tech industry came from video games. I was always curious when I went into game directory files and saw things like sprites, models and background images and wondered how it was pieced together to make the games I enjoyed. Eventually, I started watching videos on YouTube, people showing how they would make games and websites from scratch and I figured that it was something I'd enjoy doing.
